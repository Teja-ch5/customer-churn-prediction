## Business Recommendations

### 1. Contract Strategy
- **Finding:** Month-to-month customers churn **3x more** than annual contract customers
- **Action:** Offer 20% discount to switch to annual — projected retention: +12%

### 2. Early Intervention (New Customers)
- **Finding:** 65% of churners leave within the first 6 months
- **Action:** Dedicated onboarding team for customers in months 1–6

### 3. Pricing Strategy
- **Finding:** Customers paying >$70/month with no added services are highest risk
- **Action:** Offer bundled services at same price point to increase perceived value

### 4. Tech Support
- **Finding:** Customers without tech support churn **2x more**
- **Action:** Include basic tech support in all plans; upsell premium tier

### 5. Payment Method
- **Finding:** Electronic check users have 45% churn rate vs 15% for auto-pay
- **Action:** Incentivize switching to automatic payment (e.g., $5/month discount)
